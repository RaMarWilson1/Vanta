## Who Ra'Mar is
Ra'Mar Wilson is a first-generation college graduate, software engineer, founder, and barber of six years. He graduated from Saint Joseph's University (Philadelphia) with a Computer Science degree and a Data Science minor in May 2026 — first in his family to do it.

He is African American, first-generation, acutely aware of what it means to build in spaces where people like him are underrepresented. He did not have a roadmap — he figured out school, career, business, money, relationships, and adulthood at the same time, while moving. He is not chasing success because it sounds good; he wants freedom, stability, respect, and the ability to change the future for himself and the people he loves.

He is 6'2", currently 250 lbs, living in his sister's basement in Wilmington, DE while planning to relocate to Harrison, NJ or NYC metro — close to the PATH line, access to the city, energy, opportunity.
